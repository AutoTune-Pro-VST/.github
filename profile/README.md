## 🚀 Introduction
Auto-Tune Pro for Windows is the industry-standard pitch-correction and vocal-tuning software from Antares, built for producers, engineers, and singers who need dependable, musical results. Its real-time Auto mode corrects pitch as you record or perform, while its Graph mode gives you precise, graphical control over every note. Running as a plugin inside a host DAW, Antares Auto-Tune Pro fits the way you already work, whether you want quietly natural tuning or a bold creative vocal signature.

## ✨ Highlights

### Correction Tools
- **Real-time Auto mode** — Fast, low-latency pitch correction with an adjustable retune speed for anything from subtle fixes to the classic hard-tuned sound.
- **Graphical Graph mode** — Draw and refine individual notes, pitch curves, and timing for the most natural-sounding, detailed editing.
- **Key and scale control** — Constrain tuning to the song's key and scale so corrections stay musically accurate.

### Workflow and Sound
- **Natural or stylized** — Keep corrections invisible or push the settings for expressive, characterful vocal effects.
- **DAW-native operation** — Loads as a plugin so you can automate parameters and revise takes without altering the source audio.
- **Session-friendly** — Slots into your existing projects for recording, comping, and mixing vocals.

## 🎯 Getting Up and Running
1. Insert Auto-Tune Pro on the vocal channel in your DAW.
2. Choose the correct key and scale for the song.
3. Begin in Auto mode and tune the retune speed for natural or effect-heavy results.
4. Switch to Graph mode to hand-edit specific notes, pitch slides, and timing.
5. Automate parameters throughout the track to shape evolving vocal effects.
